## Overview

Conceptual and scientific background explaining *why* design tokens work the way they do. Foundations are not design systems to imitate or standards to comply with — they are the underlying models that inform good design system decisions.

**When to reach for this category:** when you need to reason from first principles rather than copy a pattern — building a novel color system, understanding why HCT produces more perceptually uniform palettes than HSL, or explaining to a team why your spacing scale uses a 4px base.
