# Typography Architecture — Principles

## Overview

How to structure a type system that works at scale — from density axis and scale construction through role taxonomy, legibility constraints, variable fonts, and the relationship between typography and spacing.

---

## What's in this section

- **architecture** — The density axis, modular vs. hand-tuned scales with ratio selection guide, named roles vs. numeric steps decision framework, non-negotiable legibility constraints (line height, measure, letter-spacing, x-height), variable font decision framework, and how typography interacts with the spacing system.
